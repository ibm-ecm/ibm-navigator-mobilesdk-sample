IBMECMFavorite

public protocol IBMECMFavorite : IBMECMBaseObject

Represents a favorite item in IBM Content Navigator. A favorite provides a quick way to access a IBMECMContentItem or a IBMECMTeamspace item. Favorites are stored as a global list on a IBMECMDesktop. Clients can present this list to users to give them access to their favorite items,

A favorite item contains its own id and name, but it also contains a link (getItemId()) back to the original item by means of a stored id. Callers can use that id and perform a retrieval or call the load() method to obtain a direct reference.
